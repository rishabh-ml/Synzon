Who We Are

Your Trusted Nutraceutical Ingredients Partner

Synzon India was founded with a singular purpose: to bridge the gap between world-class nutraceutical raw materials and the manufacturers who need them. We understand that every supplement, every functional food, and every health product starts with the quality of its ingredients.

That's why we've built a company focused entirely on sourcing, qualifying, and delivering premium ingredients — with the transparency, documentation, and reliability that pharmaceutical-grade operations demand.

Today, our catalog of 42+ ingredients serves manufacturers across 50+ countries, spanning joint support, vitamins, herbal extracts, sports nutrition, proteins, and specialty additives.
