Quality Control Process

Rigorous Testing at Every Stage

Our multi-stage quality control process ensures that every ingredient meets pharmaceutical-grade standards before it reaches your facility. From incoming raw material inspection to final pre-shipment analysis, nothing leaves our facility without thorough verification.

Vendor Qualification
Every supplier undergoes a thorough audit and qualification process, including facility inspections, documentation review, and sample testing before becoming an approved vendor.
Incoming Material Testing
Raw materials are tested upon arrival for identity, purity, potency, and contamination. Only materials passing all criteria are accepted into inventory.
Third-Party Verification
Independent, accredited third-party laboratories verify our internal test results for an additional layer of assurance and objectivity.
Batch-Level Documentation
Every batch is assigned a unique lot number with complete traceability. COA, MSDS, and spec sheets are generated and archived for every shipment.
Pre-Shipment Inspection
Final quality check before dispatch, including packaging integrity, label accuracy, and documentation completeness. Climate-controlled storage until shipping.
